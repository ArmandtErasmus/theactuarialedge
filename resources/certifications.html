Certifications for Actuaries

Recommended Certifications

These certifications are highly valued in the actuarial and finance industry.

CFA
Most Popular

Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA)

Offered by: CFA Institute

Levels: 3 levels (I, II, III)
Duration: 2.5 - 4 years average
Exam Format: Multiple choice (Level I), Item sets (Level II), Essay & Item sets (Level III)
Exam Frequency: Level I (4x/year), Level II & III (2x/year)

Pricing

  • Registration Fee: $450 (one-time)
  • Early Registration: $900 per level
  • Standard Registration: $1,200 per level
  • Total Estimated Cost: $2,700 - $4,050

Why It's Valuable for Actuaries

  • Deep understanding of investment analysis and portfolio management
  • Highly recognized in asset management and investment banking
  • Complements actuarial work in insurance and pension funds
  • Global recognition and professional network
  • Strong focus on ethics and professional standards

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience
  • Pass all three levels of exams
  • 4 years of qualified work experience
  • Become a regular member of CFA Institute

FRM
Risk Focus

Financial Risk Manager (FRM)

Offered by: Global Association of Risk Professionals (GARP)

Levels: 2 parts (Part I & Part II)
Duration: 1 - 2 years average
Exam Format: Multiple choice (both parts)
Exam Frequency: 2x/year (May & November)

Pricing

  • Enrollment Fee: $400 (one-time)
  • Early Registration: $600 per part
  • Standard Registration: $800 per part
  • Total Estimated Cost: $1,600 - $2,000

Why It's Valuable for Actuaries

  • Specialized focus on risk management and quantitative analysis
  • Directly applicable to actuarial risk modeling work
  • Strong emphasis on market risk, credit risk, and operational risk
  • Valuable for actuaries working in enterprise risk management
  • Shorter time commitment compared to CFA

Requirements

  • Pass both FRM exam parts
  • 2 years of professional work experience in financial risk management
  • Submit professional references
Visit GARP
FMVA
Financial Modeling

Financial Modeling & Valuation Analyst (FMVA)

Offered by: Corporate Finance Institute (CFI)

Format: Online self-paced program
Duration: 3 - 6 months (flexible)
Exam Format: Online assessments and final exam
Access: Lifetime access to course materials

Pricing

  • Full Program: $497 (one-time payment)
  • Monthly Subscription: $49/month
  • Annual Subscription: $297/year
  • Total Estimated Cost: $497 - $1,200

Why It's Valuable for Actuaries

  • Practical Excel and financial modeling skills
  • Valuation techniques directly applicable to actuarial work
  • Self-paced learning fits busy actuarial exam schedules
  • Hands-on experience with DCF models and M&A analysis
  • Affordable and flexible certification option
  • Great for actuaries moving into corporate finance roles

Requirements

  • Complete all required courses
  • Pass final exam with 70% or higher
  • Complete practical modeling assignments
